About ZIA

Zakaria Islamic Academy's mission is to provide all the necessary religious services that help a diverse community of believers in Allah (The Most High) and followers of the Prophet (Peace Be Upon Him) to flourish and thrive in the society we live. These services include but are not limited to communal worship, education, spiritual development, charity & service to others and the propagation of the Islamic way of life.
